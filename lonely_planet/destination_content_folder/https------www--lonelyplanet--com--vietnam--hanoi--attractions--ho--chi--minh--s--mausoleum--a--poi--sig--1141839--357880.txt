Contrary to Ho Chi Minh’s desire for a simple cremation, the mausoleum was constructed from materials gathered from all over Vietnam between 1973 and 1975. The mausoleum is usually closed from 4 September to 4 November while his embalmed body goes to Russia for maintenance.
